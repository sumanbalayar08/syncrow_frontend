WHAT IS THE SMART HOME?

A smart home refers to a regular home, which is equipped with smart devices that allow the home users to control, automate, and monitor different home fittings and devices like lighting, air conditioning, heating, shutters, curtains, media, security, and environment using their mobiles, tablets, and touch screens from anywhere over a WiFi connection.

WHAT ARE THE BENEFITS OF A SMART HOME?

A smart home enables home devices to interact together for you, to provide high levels of comfort, security, and accessibility in addition to an enjoyable living experience, thus improving your quality of life.

For example, a single tap can turn on/off lights, close or open your curtains, adjust the AC, and so on.All of this depends on your needs and your requirements.

One of the greater benefits of a smart home is that it can significantly save energy by keeping everything automated and accessible. Say you have left your home while your AC is on, you can easily adjust or turn off the AC remotely.

And how can smart home save energy? Are smart homes efficient and worth the money we spend for?

A smart home is a priceless solution for those who are seeking their comfort, peace of mind, and a futuristic lifestyle.

Efficiency is improved by optimizing the usage of all energy consuming fittings and appliances in your home to the optimum level which means no more waste of energy.

Lighting, air conditioning, water heater, shutters and curtains are controlled efficiently to maintain the energy consumption at an optimal level.

Lights could be dimmed to support the natural light maintaining the wellbeing illumination level in the room. Air conditioning could be programmed to turn On or Off and to adjust temperature as per your lifestyle and schedule. Even a water heater can be scheduled as per your daily needs.
